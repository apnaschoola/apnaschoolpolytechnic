Addmission Procedure

Admissions to courses are offered by entrance exam conducted by Joint entrance Examination Council Lucknow (http://www.jeecup.org). Entrance Exam forms
are available on ( jeecup.nic.in ) from 15th dec to 15 Jan , the JEE exam is conducted on Sunday of April. Admissions to part time courses are offered by entrance
test/interview conducted at institute Level in the month of july/August.
